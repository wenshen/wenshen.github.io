About Me

I completed my PhD in Information and Computer Science at the University of California, Irvine, where I was very fortunate to work with Prof. Cristina Lopes. Prior to UCI, I obtained a M.Sc. in Computing and Information Science and a B.Eng. in Software Engineering from Masdar Institute of Science & Technology and Northwestern Polytechnical University, respectively. For more details, please see my CV.
